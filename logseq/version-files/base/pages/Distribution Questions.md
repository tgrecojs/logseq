- #### Calculation of \( a_1 \) for Each Tier
  
  We use the formula:
  $$
  a_1 = \frac{\text{Total Tokens per Tier}}{\text{Number of Individuals} \cdot \frac{1 - r^5}{1 - r}}
  $$

  $$ a_1 = \frac{5,000,000}{1,000 \cdot 3.3616} \approx 1,487.90 $$
